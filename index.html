Aarhus summer trip (image experiment)

Today I want to simply do one experiment of posting pictures in the blog.

The picture was taken in ARoS Aarhus Kunstmuseum’s characteristic colorful path at its roof. The time was summer in 2025.

Aarhus is a pretty city. It has nice food, good transportation, tourist friendly road signs, and affordable accommodation. ARoS gave me quite a lovely exhibition of installation art. Host at Airbnb gave me a nice room and interesting conversations.

One amazing Danish dish I wanted to talk about here is called Stegt Flæsk(‘pork strips’ or ‘crisp fried pork slices’). For a poor student here I feel very satisfied with it. It is affordable, tasty, containing sufficient fat and oil. Still the salty taste is not vulgar. The plating is like typically houseman’s dish but with some simple passion to food.
